## Overview

The Snowflake Equation Lab is a behavioral diagnostic instrument combining experimental political science, organizational behavior, and clinical behavioral therapy frameworks. It diagnoses foundational mindset architecture, identity threat patterns, and structural force dynamics.

## The Kenney 2x2 Matrix

|                   | Narrow Diet              | Diverse Diet                          |
|-------------------|--------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| **Rigid Mindset** | Q1: Detectable tribalism | Q2: Sophisticated tribalism (hardest) |
| **Flexible Mindset** | Q3: Recoverable       | Q4: Target state                      |

## Q2 Applied to Management

The control-dependency manager is the canonical Q2 organizational case: diverse information diet (attended training, conferences, certifications), rigid foundational mindset (burned in from first 5-10 years), auxiliary hypothesis (every counter-evidence absorbed and neutralized).

## The Kenney Rule

35 years of management experience in a broken feedback loop does not produce 35 years of learning. It produces 3-5 years of learning repeated 7-10 times, with deepening certainty.
