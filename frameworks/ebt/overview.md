# Evaluative Bias Transference (EBT) Framework v1.2
*Coined: Erwin Maurice McDonald | February 25, 2026*

## Overview

EBT is a PhD-grade organizational behavior framework diagnosing the structural process by which human evaluative bias transfers into organizational systems — including AI systems, promotion pipelines, and leadership development programs — encodes as objective criteria, and re-emerges in a form that is structurally resistant to challenge.

## Three Pathways

**Pathway 1 — Training Data Contamination**
Historically biased human decisions become the dataset. Organizations learn to replicate the decision pattern as optimal.

**Pathway 2 — Structural Criteria Encoding (SCE)**
Non-representative development teams embed non-representative performance proxies, encoded as objective evaluation criteria before a single candidate is assessed.

**Pathway 3 — Objectivity Laundering**
Bias stops being a judgment and starts being called data. The tools for contesting judgment no longer apply. Structural unchallengability is confirmed.

## Applied to Leadership Development

The leadership development industry operates as an organizational Objectivity Laundering system: completion metrics (attendance, assessment scores) are laundered into transformation claims (leadership capability developed) through a credentialing system that no organization is structurally incentivized to audit.
