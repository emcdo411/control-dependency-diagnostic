## What This Is

This repository contains an integrated diagnostic framework built using AI to identify, name, and structurally explain why control-dependency management styles persist in organizations — despite decades of leadership development investment — and why the industry designed to fix them is structurally incentivized not to.

**This is not a critique of individual managers. It is a structural systems diagnosis.**

The $366B leadership development industry survives on a fundamental deception: it measures what is easy (attendance, knowledge scores) and calls it what organizations need (behavioral transformation, culture change). The research has known this for 40 years. The industry does not cite it in sales materials.

## Frameworks

<details>
<summary><strong>EBT Framework v1.2 — Evaluative Bias Transference</strong></summary>

Three pathways by which human evaluative bias transfers into organizational systems and becomes structurally unchallengeable:

- **Pathway 1 — Training Data Contamination:** Historically biased decisions become the dataset. The system learns to replicate bias as optimal.
- **Pathway 2 — Structural Criteria Encoding (SCE):** Non-representative promotion criteria encode non-representative performance proxies as objective evaluation standards.
- **Pathway 3 — Objectivity Laundering:** Bias stops being a judgment and starts being called data. The tools for contesting judgment no longer apply.

*Coined: Erwin Maurice McDonald | February 25, 2026*
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Snowflake Equation Lab v3 — Behavioral Diagnostics</strong></summary>

Behavioral diagnostic instrument for foundational mindset architecture, identity threat, and structural force dynamics.

**The Kenney Rule:** Experience in a broken feedback loop produces pattern calcification, not learning. 35 years of management under a control-dependency style does not produce 35 years of wisdom — it produces 3-5 years of pattern formation repeated 7-10 times with deepening certainty.

*Built on: Westwood · Edmondson · ACT/DBT/MI · Kenney · Bandura*
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Research Analyzer v3 — Structural Intelligence Edition</strong></summary>

PhD-grade research instrument fusing empirical rigor, IMD/Toegel OB science, EBT bias detection, and Long Horizon Intelligence.

**The Myth Audit finding on leadership development:** Knowledge gains reliable. Behavioral change weak and non-durable. Organizational performance impact near-zero. The industry does not cite this in sales materials.

*Built on: Burke & Day (1986) · Collins & Holton (2004) · Toegel · Heilman · Westwood*
</details>
